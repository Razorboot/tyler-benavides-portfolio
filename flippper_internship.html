Flippper Internship Experience

By: Tyler Benavides | At: Flippper, | Founded By: Sabine Braunschweig

Showcase

During this internship, I created a collection of 3D models and animations which allowed investors to visualize the products they might be funding. This demos resulted in Sabine Braunschweig, the founder of Flippper, to secure an official partnership with a major German education research institute, resulting in an official patent to be awarded for the design of the Flippper notebook.
